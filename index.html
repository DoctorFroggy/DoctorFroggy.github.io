Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 4, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Drive Coach ("the App"), provided by Benjamin Interrante, handles your information.

The short version

Drive Coach is built to keep your data on your device. Your drives, routes, and statistics are stored locally on your phone. We do not operate servers that collect your location or driving data, and we do not sell your data to anyone.

1. Location data

The App uses your device's location (GPS), including in the background while a drive is active, for one purpose: to record your drive so it can calculate and show you your time and fuel saved or lost, and your route.

  • Location tracking runs only while a drive is active (between when you tap "Start Drive" and "End Drive").
  • Your location and drive data are stored locally on your device.
  • We do not transmit your location data to our own servers. (We don't operate servers that receive it.)

2. What we store, and where

Recorded drives — including GPS points, speed, timestamps, route, and the resulting statistics — are stored locally on your device. If you delete the App, this data is removed with it.

3. Third-party services

The App may use third-party services that have their own privacy practices:

  • Apple / App Store and TestFlight — for distribution, and Apple may collect standard app analytics and crash data. See Apple's privacy policy.
  • Map display — the App uses Apple Maps to display your route. Apple's privacy policy applies to map usage.
  • Advertising (when added) — Once advertising is added, an ad provider (such as Google AdMob) may collect device and usage information to serve ads. This section will be updated at that time, and you will be shown any required consent prompts (such as Apple's App Tracking Transparency request).
  • Speed-limit data — The App uses road and speed-limit data derived from OpenStreetMap, bundled into the App. This does not involve sending your location to OpenStreetMap.

4. What we do NOT do

  • We do not sell your personal data.
  • We do not share your drive or location data with advertisers or data brokers.
  • We do not require you to create an account or provide personal information to use the core App.

5. Data you choose to share

When you use the "share this drive" feature, you create an image and choose whether and with whom to share it, through your device's normal share options. That's entirely under your control.

6. Children

The App is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from them.

7. Your choices

  • You can turn off location permission in your device settings at any time (though the App won't function without it).
  • You can delete all your data by deleting the App.

8. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy. Material changes will be reflected by updating the effective date, and where appropriate, an in-App notice.
